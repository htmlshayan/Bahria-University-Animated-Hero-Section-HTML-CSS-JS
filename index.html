How To Apply

Step 1: CLICK HERE TO APPLY NOW and Create an Account

CLICK HERE For International Students

Step 2: Check your email, Click on the link received in the email and set your Password

Step 3: Log in to your BU account with your email and password (Remember your password)

Step 4: Fill online form following each given step & generate a Fee Voucher

Step 5: Take a print of the Fee Voucher and Pay the Admissions Processing Fee in any branch of Alfalah or Allied Bank

Step 6: Print Entry Test Slip (Admit Slip)

Step 7: Appear in Computer Based Test (CBTs)

Step 8: Find your name in Merit List uploaded by the University on www.bahria.edu.pk

Step 9: Appear in the Interview as per the date given against your name

Step 10: Submit Admissions Fee (1st Semester)
